## 今日趋势观察

1. Agent accountability is becoming a product category: Brain0, Fable Soul, Hunch and Agent BOM all focus on why an agent acted, what it touched, and whether the output is trustworthy.
2. Local-first AI workspaces are moving beyond chat logs into project materials, collaborators, evidence and portable backups; Polaris is the clearest fresh signal today.
3. MCP infrastructure is splitting into practical local toolboxes, proxy studios, rate-limit operations and domain-specific servers rather than just connector demos.
4. Mobile embedded agents are emerging as a separate surface from web/desktop chat; Napaxi is notable because it frames sandboxed mobile agent execution as an SDK.
5. Vertical AI products remain interesting when they package a complete workflow, as seen in e-commerce image generation and agentic trading, but they need stronger runtime validation before broad adoption.
