## 今日趋势观察

1. Local native AI apps are back as a strong product shape: Nativ turns local MLX chat/serving/monitoring into a Mac app rather than a CLI-only workflow.
2. Agent trust infrastructure is splitting into authorization, transactional effects, semantic records and evidence-backed payments; AEP, AgentKernel, Kin and AIPAY show adjacent primitives.
3. Security is becoming a natural vertical for agents because orchestration, evidence and review loops can be inspected; Open Kritt is the clearest fresh example.
4. Multi-agent work is moving from prompt packs to operator surfaces: BossConsole, Agents Council and RVMP all try to make concurrent agent work visible and routable.
5. Cross-channel and local-first vertical apps are practical AI-native entry points, especially study workspaces, agent identities and browser/control tools.
