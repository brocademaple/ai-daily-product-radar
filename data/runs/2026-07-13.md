## 今日趋势观察

1. Governed coding-agent IDEs are moving from chat wrappers to release-evidence workbenches: Clodex leads because it exposes app/package/docs/security/release surfaces around autonomous execution.
2. Agent runtimes are becoming operational products: Managed Agents, Recensa and PlanDeck all make sessions, audit, replay, approvals or plan state visible.
3. Vertical agent apps are more compelling than generic assistants when they own a workflow: Morphe AI, Guido, line-summary and Callhome each bind AI to a concrete environment.
4. MCP remains the dominant packaging layer for local private tools, especially chat history, search, legislative records and reverse engineering.
5. Creative/media agent skills continue to grow, but repeated coverage requires new runtime evidence, not only extra stars.
